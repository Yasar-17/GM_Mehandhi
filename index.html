About US

Clean, neat and patient work

Gulnaz Banu is a friendly mehandhi artist who loves drawing fine mehendi lines and balanced patterns that look simple yet detailed.

She listens to what you like, checks your dress colours and suggests designs that fit your style, whether you want very heavy work or light and minimal.

Neat finishing

Sharp cones and steady hands so the designs look clear even in close photos.

Comfortable sitting

Easy, relaxed talks and breaks when needed so you do not feel tired during long bridal sessions.
